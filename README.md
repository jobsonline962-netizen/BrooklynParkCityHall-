# BrooklynParkCityHall-


## About Brooklyn Park City Hall

Welcome to the heart of our community. <a href="https://BrooklynParkCityHall.com">**BrooklynParkCityHall**</a> is more than just a government building; it is the hub for local innovation, resident services, and the daily operations that keep the **sixth-largest city in Minnesota** thriving.

Located at **5200 85th Ave. N.**, City Hall serves as the central point for our Mayor, City Council, and the dedicated departments that work tirelessly to ensure Brooklyn Park remains a "Jewel of the North."

### Our Mission and Vision

At the City of Brooklyn Park, our mission is clear: **We are a thriving community inspiring pride where opportunities exist for all.** We operate under a set of core values that guide every decision made within these walls:

* **Inclusion:** We value all people and diverse ideas.
* **Partnership:** We build trusting relationships with residents and stakeholders.
* **Accountability:** We are responsible stewards of the community’s resources.
* **Learning:** We strive for continuous improvement and innovation every day.

### Services Provided at City Hall

Brooklyn Park City Hall is your one-stop destination for essential local government services. Whether you are a long-time resident or a new business owner, we are here to assist you with:

* **City Council Meetings:** Participate in local governance and stay informed on city ordinances.
* **DMV Services:** Conveniently located inside City Hall for your licensing and registration needs.
* **Permits & Licensing:** Access resources for building permits, business licenses, and zoning inquiries.
* **Utility & Billing:** Pay your water bills and manage city services.
* **Elections & Voting:** Get information on polling places and voter registration.

### A Thriving, Diverse Community

With a population of over **86,000 residents**, Brooklyn Park is proud to be one of the most diverse cities in Minnesota. City Hall is committed to ensuring that our services are accessible to everyone. We offer resources in multiple languages and programs like **"City Hall on the Go"** to meet our residents where they are.

From our 2,000 acres of parkland to our bustling commercial districts, Brooklyn Park City Hall is dedicated to protecting our history—rooted in our famous potato-farming heritage—while building a sustainable, prosperous future for the next generation.
